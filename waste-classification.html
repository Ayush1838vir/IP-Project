AI Smart Bins

Our AI-powered waste classification system uses computer vision to automatically sort waste into appropriate categories. This technology helps reduce contamination in recycling streams and ensures proper disposal of hazardous materials.

Biodegradable

Food scraps, garden waste, and organic materials

Recyclable

Paper, glass, plastics, and metals

Hazardous

Chemicals, batteries, and toxic materials

Electronic Waste

Computers, smartphones, and electronic devices

How It Works

Image Recognition

Our AI system uses advanced image recognition algorithms to identify different types of waste materials with high accuracy.

Real-time Processing

The system processes images in real-time, providing immediate feedback on waste classification to users.

Continuous Learning

Our AI models continuously learn from new data, improving classification accuracy over time.

Integration with Smart Bins

The AI system is integrated with our smart bins, automatically directing waste to the appropriate container.
